# 🌱 EcoRoute – Travel Smart, Travel Green  
*An Eco-Friendly Commute Awareness Web App (Frontend Prototype, In Progress)*  

**EcoRoute** is a web-based platform designed to encourage sustainable travel choices. By highlighting **eco-friendly routes** and raising awareness of **CO₂ emissions**, it empowers users to balance **time, cost, health, and environmental impact**.  

Whether you're commuting daily or planning occasional trips, EcoRoute helps you travel smarter — with **eco-awareness, comfort, and responsibility**.  

---

## 🌿 Key Features (Planned)

- 🚶 **Eco-Friendly Routing** – Walking, cycling, carpooling, and public transport options  
- 🌍 **CO₂ Emission Awareness** – Estimated carbon footprint per route  
- 🎮 **Gamification** – Badges, leaderboards, and rewards for green choices  
- 🗺️ **Interactive Map UI** – Highlighting green routes and bicycle-friendly paths  
- 🌦️ **Dynamic Suggestions** – Routes adjusted based on traffic & weather (future)  
- 🌳 **Extra Eco Features** – Virtual tree plantation, eco alerts, EV charging stop mockups  
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Mobile-first experience with dark/light mode  
- 🌐 **Multi-language Support (Planned)** – Accessible to diverse users
